About Sturbee Financial Coaching

A calm, structured space to make money decisions you’ll feel good about.

I’m Brian Sturkey, a Master Financial Coach. I help busy professionals, couples, and families create a clear, realistic plan for their money so they can move from stress and drift to confidence and intention.

My approach is straightforward: no jargon, no judgment, and no product sales. Just practical coaching rooted in proven principles, tailored to your real life and your real numbers.

FAQ

Common questions.

Is this the same as investment advice or financial planning?

No. I don’t manage investments, sell financial products, or provide tax/legal advice. Coaching focuses on your cash flow, spending, debt, savings, and practical day-to-day decisions. When needed, I’ll encourage you to work with qualified tax, legal, or investment professionals as well.

What if I’m embarrassed about my situation?

You’re not alone and you’re not the worst I’ve ever seen. Coaching is private, judgment-free, and focused on where you’re going next, not where you’ve been. The fact that you’re exploring this is already a strong step.

Do you work with couples?

Yes. Working with couples is a significant part of my coaching. We’ll create room for both perspectives and build a plan you can both support, even if you approach money differently.

How are sessions held?

Most sessions are held virtually via Zoom. Before we meet, you’ll complete a brief questionnaire and, if you’re comfortable, share some basic numbers so we can use our time efficiently and focus on decisions not data entry.
